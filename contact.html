Submit a Story Tip

Have a story we should cover? We're always looking for newsworthy developments, trending topics, and important stories that matter to our readers. Send your tips to editorial@newstrendings.com with "STORY TIP" in the subject line.

Please include:

  • A brief description of the story
  • Why you think it's newsworthy
  • Any relevant sources, links, or contacts
  • Your contact information (we respect confidentiality when requested)

Report an Error

Accuracy is fundamental to our mission. If you've spotted an error, factual inaccuracy, or outdated information in any of our articles, please let us know immediately. We review all error reports and issue corrections when warranted.

Email corrections to editorial@newstrendings.com with "CORRECTION" in the subject line. Please include:

  • The article URL
  • The specific error or inaccuracy
  • The correct information (with sources if available)
